My friends and I worked on developing a mobile app that provides an easy to use and convenient photo sharing service. Compass

February 2019

For a hackathon, I created a travel app with personalized itineraries to make planning trips easier. This app was created to avoid having numerous tabs opened and excel sheets created when planning a trip. The app focuses on proving the user a platform to create their own itinerary based off of search results (instead of having to manually input details) while also having in depth details of the planned destination or activity without being obstructed by varying reviews and feedback.
